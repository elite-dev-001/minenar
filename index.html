What is Minenar

Minenar is a staking platform in the DeFi ecosystem for LTC. Minenar lets users stake their LTC by locking assets and maintaining infrastructure. Minenar attempts to solve the problems associated with staking platforms- illiquidity, immovability, and accessibility and improve the security of the Litecoin network. We aim to eradicate the inconsistency and fear factors that have been conceptualized in the Defi ecosystem

How does Minenar work?

When staking with Minenar, users receive additional Litecoin and Litecoin cash bonuses, and are updated daily to reflect your Litecoin staking rewards. Note that there are lock-ups and a minimum stake amount of 2.5 LTC when staking with Minenar.

When using Minenar, users receive secure staking rewards in real-time, allowing for participation in the securing of Litecoin with fewer associated risks and less downside potential.

Can I use the Minenar platform without KYC?

All new users are required to complete the verification process to access Minenar service offerings including purchasing the minter, deposits, staking, and withdrawals.

How secure is Minenar?

The contracts in Minenar are fast, scalable, and secured. It is protected with the 256-bit SHA hash functions. Unlike encryption which can be decrypted, the SHA hash functions provide a unique fingerprint for each transaction that can not be reconstructed. Minenar is a secure staking platform for some reasons: Open-sourcing & continuous review of all code. Committee of elected, best-in-class validators to minimize staking risk. Use of non-custodial staking service to eliminate counterparty risk. Use of Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) for governance decisions & to manage risk factors.

How much does any party have over the user's funds?

Funds are deposited to the Minenar smart contract and then are locked into the minting pool. The threshold signature account is collectively controlled by the signatories chosen by the Minenar Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO).

How long will deposit and withdrawal take?

All deposit and withdrawal transactions are processed automatically on the Minenar platform. If deposit and withdrawal are not confirmed within 24 hours, please email us at info@minenar.com and our customer service will respond to you as soon as possible.
